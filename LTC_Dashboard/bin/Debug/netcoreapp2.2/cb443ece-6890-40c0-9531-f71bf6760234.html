Smart Snacks for Healthier Teeth

Your whole life, you’ve probably known that you need to brush your teeth soon after eating to help cut down on your cavity risk.

But could some foods actually be good enough for your teeth, that you don’t have to rush off to the sink right away after your meal?

It turns out that yes, some foods can actually help strengthen and “clean” your smile while you eat them, making them ideal for snacks throughout the day (when you’re not able to go rush right away.) That being in mind, don’t use them in lieu of your toothbrush and floss.

Here are a few examples:

Fresh Apples, Carrots, and Celery — Crispy and fibrous, eating fresh fruits and veggies help wipe your teeth clean of plaque while stimulating blood flow in your gums.

Sharp Cheddar Cheese — The pH level of sharp cheeses can neutralize acidic biofilm inside your mouth. Plus, the calcium inside of it is great for healthy teeth and bones!

Popcorn — Unless you’re prone to getting the hulls stuck between your teeth and gums, air-popped popcorn is healthy for your body and great for your teeth, similar to the way fresh produce is.
